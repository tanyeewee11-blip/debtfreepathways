Understanding your debt numbers

Most people know roughly how much they owe — but very few know their exact payoff date, total interest cost, or how much a small extra payment would change things. That gap between knowing your balance and understanding your debt is exactly what these tools are designed to close.

A $10,000 credit card balance at 22% APR with minimum payments will take over 20 years to pay off and cost more than $15,000 in interest alone. Increasing that payment by $150/month cuts the timeline to under 5 years and saves over $12,000. Most people do not know these numbers — and once they do, they make different decisions.

That is the purpose of DebtFreePathways: not to tell you what to do, but to give you the exact numbers so you can decide for yourself.
