About DebtFreePathways

Free financial calculators, built for real people.

DebtFreePathways was built with one goal: give everyone access to the same financial tools that used to require a financial advisor or expensive software.

We offer six free calculators — debt payoff, mortgage, loan amortization, interest rate comparison, debt avalanche vs snowball, and emergency fund — all running entirely in your browser. No account needed, no data collected, no ads cluttering your results.

Whether you're trying to pay off a credit card, figure out your mortgage payment, or build an emergency fund, our tools give you clear, accurate numbers so you can make better financial decisions.

Our principles

  • Free, always. Every calculator is completely free. No premium plan, no paywall.
  • Private by design. All calculations happen in your browser. We never store or share your data.
  • No fluff. Just clear inputs and honest results.
  • Not financial advice. Our tools help you understand your numbers — for advice tailored to your situation, consult a qualified financial advisor.
